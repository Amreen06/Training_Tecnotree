Yamunotri Dham is part of Char Dham – the four famous Hindu pilgrimages in the Himalayas.
The other famous Hindu pilgrimages in the Himalayas are Gangotri, Kedarnath and Badrinath.
Yamunotri Dham is the starting point of the annual pilgrimage that is held from May to October.
It is situated close to River Yamuna’s source.
