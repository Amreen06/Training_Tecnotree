Tribute to Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is one of the most renowned cricketers in the world. He is the current captain of the Indian cricket team and has won numerous accolades throughout his career. Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, Kohli showed an interest in cricket at a very young age. He started playing for his school and quickly gained recognition for his talent. Kohli made his international debut for the Indian cricket team in 2008. He made an immediate impact, scoring 18 runs off 10 balls in his first match against Sri Lanka. Since then, he has become a vital part of the Indian cricket team and has been an integral part of many important victories for the country. He has played in all three formats of the game, including Tests, One Day Internationals (ODIs), and T20 Internationals.

Accomplishments

  • Fastest to 12,000 ODI runs
  • Most centuries in successful run chases
  • Second highest number of centuries in ODI cricket
  • Most runs in a single IPL season
  • ICC Cricketer of the Year (2017, 2018)
